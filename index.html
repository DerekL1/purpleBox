Glass is, in many places, not accepted in curbside recycling programs. This is due to the costs and contamination that glass recycling causes.

As a result, the two methods of glass disposal are either to carefully package it into the trash, or drop it off at purple glass-only recycling containers.

Recycling glass is preferable to trashing it, but many people do not know about the Purple Box Glass Recycling program, or where they are located.

Use this website to easily find where the nearest Purple Box location is based off of current driving conditions.
